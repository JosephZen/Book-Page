🌈 Challenge 3: The Rainbow Trap

Master Pixel presented a design bursting with every color imaginable. It looked like a unicorn sneezed on it.
Lina cringed. Tip #3: Stick to a Cohesive Color Palette. “Too many colors can overwhelm. Choose 2–3 primary colors and complementary shades for balance.”
Master Pixel smiled. She was learning.
🔠 Challenge 4: The Font Frenzy

Now came a flyer with a dozen different fonts. Some were curly, others bold, and one suspiciously looked like Comic Sans.
Lina chuckled. Tip #4: Limit Your Fonts. “Two fonts max—one for headings, one for body text. Consistency keeps it clean.”
Master Pixel chuckled too (though he secretly liked Comic Sans, but he’d never admit it).
