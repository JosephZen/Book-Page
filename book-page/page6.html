💡 Challenge 7: The Fear of Feedback

For the final challenge, Master Pixel said nothing. He simply handed Lina her own design, waiting silently.
At first, Lina felt nervous. But then she realized: Tip #7: Don’t Fear Feedback. “Design is a process,” she whispered. “Feedback helps us grow. It’s not about being perfect—it’s about getting better.”
Master Pixel grinned. “You’ve learned well, Lina.”
