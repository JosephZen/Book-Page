🗒️ Challenge 1: The Curse of Clutter

Master Pixel handed Lina a poster crowded with text, images, and random doodles. “What’s wrong with this design?” he asked.
Lina squinted. Then it hit her. Tip #1: Embrace White Space. “Design needs breathing room,” she said. “White space isn’t empty—it helps important elements stand out.”
Master Pixel nodded.

🎯 Challenge 2: The Lost Focus
Next, he showed her a website with buttons scattered everywhere. “Where should I click?” he grumbled.
Lina realized the problem. Tip #2: Establish Visual Hierarchy. “Use size, color, and contrast to guide the viewer’s eye,” she said. “The most important stuff should pop first.”
Another nod from the master.
